Intro to Programming - Investigate a Dataset¶

To complete the project i chose the Gapminder-World-Dataset. Since global warming is a major topic of our time, i focused on this topic as an overarching theme, and took a deeper look at the C02 - dataset regarding the following questions:

  • Which country has the highest C02-emission, compared to the population since 1945?
  • Is there a correlation between population growth and C02-emission growth?
  • Is there a correlation between higher gas prices and lower C02 emission?

Is there a correlation between population growth and C02-emission growth?¶

To answer this question the function corrwith() is used, which calculates the correlation between two DataFrames, matching column- and index names. Axis='columns' is chosen to get the correlation for each country, and not for each year

In [59]:
